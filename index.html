HACKATHON

October 9, 2015
Wellesley College

Awesome women making awesome things.

W&S Hack is a hackathon for awesome women in the Boston area who like to make cool things. The main goal is to encourage learning new technologies and to provide networking opportunities between students and industry professionals by having integrated teams.

In order to effectively foster a unique learning opportunity and a networking oriented experience, teams of 4-5 will be composed of a Wellesley/Simmons student host (to provide living accommodations), students from other colleges, and a team mentor. This team construction will create an equal playing field for each team and allow for intercollegiate networking.

Who is this for and how can I join?

The W&S hackathon is focused on creating a space for undergraduate women in the New England area to learn, network, and build. We are focused on supporting women in tech, and on creating an environment that is beginner-friendly.

Register here :) No experience required!

FAQ

  • Q: I am a noob! I don't know how to do anything!
    A: Don't worry! Each team will have a mentor, so THIS EVENT IS WHERE YOU CAN FEARLESSLY LEARN/BUILD THINGS. Beginners are welcome.

  • Q: Is this event only for women?
    A: This event is geared toward women. If you are not a woman and would like to participate, please feel free to fill out the registration form. Keep in mind that we are looking to create a safe space. If you have any questions or thoughts about it, please contact us!

  • Q: Can I hack with my friends?
    A: Our aim is to strengthen the network of women in CS, and we hope to achieve this by creating "random" teams where each team will have 1 Wellesley/Simmons student and 1 mentor. If you MUST hack with a friend, please stipulate so in the registration form, and we'll do our best to accommodate!

  • Q: Do I need to have a finished project by the end?
    A: Ideally you'd have a finished/close-to-finished project by Sunday. If you don't finish, not a bad thing! We won't catapult you to Pluto. Ultimately, this is a learning experience, and we want this to be fun for everyone!

  • Q: I'm in X Y Z events that will take up a small portion of my weekend, but I would still like to participate. Is that okay?
    A: Please stipulate this in your registration form. We would love to have you come for at least part of the hackathon, and we'll do the best we can to accommodate this when constructing teams.

  • Q: Are there travel reimbursements available?
    A: Yes! Please fill out the form, and we will handle each case individually.

Contact us via EMAIL or TWITTER if you have any questions.
