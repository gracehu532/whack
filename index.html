FAQ

    What is a hackathon?
    Itâ€™s a grand gathering of people who get their hands dirty and program together. Try out new technologies, practice a new language, and implement something thatâ€™s funky, exciting, and maybe just for kicks and giggles.
    What if I have ZERO experience?
    Thatâ€™s absolutely fine! We encourage beginners to join us. Get psyched to learn a ton from our mentors and have a blast with your teammates!
    Is this event only for women?
    This event is geared toward women. If you are not a woman and would like to participate, please feel free to fill out the registration form. Keep in mind that we are looking to create a safe space. If you have any questions or thoughts about it, please contact us!
    Can I hack with my friends?
    Our aim is to strengthen the network of women in CS, and we hope to achieve this by creating "random" teams where each team will have 1 student and 1 mentor. If you MUST hack with a friend, please stipulate so in the registration form, and we'll do our best to accommodate!
    Do I need to have a finished project by the end?
    Ideally you'd have a finished/close-to-finished project by Sunday. If you don't finish, not a bad thing! We won't catapult you to Pluto. Ultimately, this is a learning experience, and we want this to be fun for everyone!
    What if I can only come for part of the weekend?
    Please stipulate this in your registration form. We would love to have you come for at least part of the hackathon, and we'll do the best we can to accommodate this when constructing teams.
    Are there travel reimbursements available?
    Yes! Please fill out the form, and we will handle each case individually.
    Have other questions?
    Contact us at hack@wellesley.edu and tweet at us at @wellesleyhack!
